Purpose

Whether you need to generate ideas to expand your portfolio, my design brief can help. I created this to have a simple, minimalist brief generator that doesn't require you to log in, has no pop-ups, and no payments. Enjoy!

How to use

It is a minimalist brief generator, coded to allow you to practise and give you ideas for interfaces to create for your portfolio projects. It is free and open to use. You can find out more about my work on my website.
