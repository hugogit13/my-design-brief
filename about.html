The difficulty of finding ideas for screen concepts has always been something I've struggled with. My case study is not a miracle solution; there are many other brief generators out there, but if it helps you get that little spark that will give you an idea, then this little site will have served its purpose. Welcome, thank you for using my case study. It is a minimalist brief generator, coded to allow you to practise and give you ideas for interfaces to create for your portfolio projects. It is free and open to use. You can find out more about my work on my website.
